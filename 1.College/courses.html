Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Recognized Bachelor’s Degree of minimum 3 years duration. The Candidates need to have passed the prescribed qualifying exam with not less than 50% marks in aggregate of all years of the degree examinations. 50% of the total intake to be filled by PGCET and 50% by the Management.

List of MBA Courses offered :

  1. MBA (Marketing /HR /Finance electives)
  2. MBA (Corporate Finance)
  3. MBA (Retail Management)
  4. MBA (Executive Program – 3 years)
  5. MBA (Digital Marketing)

Master of Computer Applications (MCA)

Recognized Bachelor’s Degree of minimum 3 years duration with Mathematics at 10+2 level. The Candidates need to have passed the prescribed qualifying exam with not less than 50% marks in aggregate of all years of the degree examinations. 50% of the total intake to be filled by PGCET and 50% by the Management.

Master of Technology (M.Tech)

Open to all students who have passed B.E / B.Tech examination / AMIE(relevant field ) of VTU or any other university/institution. The Candidates need to have passed the prescribed qualifying exam with not less than 50% marks in aggregate of all years of the degree examinations. The details of Admission of Government seats is available at http://kea.kar.nic.in

PGCET College Code – M.Tech college code T873 , MCA C406
